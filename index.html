About Tuta

From the Turkish word "tutmak" meaning "to hold" — Tuta was designed to simplify how you carry essentials.

All you need, nothing more.

Born from the frustration of cluttered bags and the need for quick access to essentials, Tuta combines functional design with sustainable materials to create the perfect everyday companion.

Whether you're hiking in nature, navigating a busy campus, or simply want to declutter your daily routine, Tuta's modular approach to carrying gives you exactly what you need — with none of the excess.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Tuta.

What bottle sizes does Tuta fit?

Tuta is designed to fit most standard bottles and thermoses between 500ml and 1.5L. The adjustable clip mechanism ensures a secure fit regardless of bottle diameter.

How do I clean my Tuta strap?

Tuta can be hand washed with mild soap and water. Allow it to air dry completely before reattaching to your bottle. We recommend avoiding machine washing to maintain the quality of materials.

What materials is Tuta made from?

Tuta is crafted from a thoughtful blend of sustainable and high-quality materials, including recycled polyester, organic cotton, and eco-friendly hardware. It also features premium organic leather accents on the tips and durable metal attachments for added strength. Our stitching is of exceptional quality, ensuring long-lasting durability. Packaging is kept minimal and made from recycled paper to reduce environmental impact.

How long will shipping take?

Standard shipping within Turkey takes 2-4 business days. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout.

Can I return my Tuta if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we offer a 14-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with your Tuta, you can return it in its original condition within 14 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.
